## Research Areas

Our group focuses on research in geotechnical engineering, computational mechanics, and biomechanics. We employ numerical methods, experimental testing, and machine learning approaches to solve complex engineering problems.

### Key Research Topics

- Computational methods for geotechnical engineering
- Biomechanics and bio-inspired materials
- Numerical modeling of granular materials
- Machine learning applications in civil engineering
- Hydrate-bearing sediments and subsurface characterization
